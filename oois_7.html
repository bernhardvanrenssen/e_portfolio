Normailsation of Property Rental Tables

Below the tables in 3NF for the PropertyRental exercise. The fields that are in bold act as the Primary Key for each entity. Where multiple fields are in bold, the Primary Key is a Composite Key.
An assumption made is that the field Street is a unique street adress with street number, street name and city. For the purpose of this exercise, these fields are not further divided into separate fields, but remain as one field with multiple values to form a unique entry.
