Both pieces of code have functions that contain a complexity of 1. This is due to the number of decisions or events that happen in each function. Both functions only have the “Assert” statement to run, giving them their respective cyclomatic complexity. The final line is then the total complexity of running all the code, where sums2 produces a complexity of 2, and sums produces a 1.
